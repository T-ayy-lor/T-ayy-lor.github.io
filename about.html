about

Hello world, I've created this space to serve as a garden for all my interests. I'm largely inspired by techincal applications for artistic expression, and I'm hoping this platform will allow for such interests to grow and develop. I think it'll be a fun way to document all my endeavors, nonetheless. Since I've left social media, and for a multitude of associated reasons, I largely feel like a lone ranger both in the tech industry and online in general. So, this project is my love letter to what the web used to be, the wild west.
